ABOUT

My approach to tech
and helping build the products and engineers of tomorrow.

I believe that tech should be made accessible, both from a consumer standpoint, as well as in terms of entry to work in the industry. When in an engineering capacity I strive to build products that are intuitive and beneficial to the consumer. When teaching, I believe in providing as many hands on development opportunities as possible, focusing on directly applicable skills and computer science theory. In both capacities, I aim to foster a culture that encourages asking as many questions as possible and allowing margin to fail in order to grow. No one is perfect and we need to achieve a balance of making tech efficient as well as human.
